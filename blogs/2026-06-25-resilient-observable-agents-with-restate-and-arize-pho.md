This article explains how combining Restate and Arize Phoenix enables production-ready agents by providing durable execution that recovers from failures and comprehensive observability through traces. The integration lets teams execute durably, trace every step, evaluate outputs, annotate failures, and use those signals to improve the next version, with version-pinned traces and asynchronous evaluations.
